Bir Billing

India’s Paragliding Paradise in the Himalayas – Soar Above the Clouds

About Bir Billing

Located in Himachal Pradesh, Bir Billing is world-renowned for paragliding. It offers one of the best take-off and landing sites in the world, nestled in the Dhauladhar ranges at 2400 meters.

Fly the Himalayas

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush as you glide across valleys and peaks in Bir Billing.

The site hosted the first-ever Paragliding World Cup in India in 2015, drawing pilots from over 140 countries.

Why It’s Famous

Bir Billing has a perfect mix of thermals, cloud base height, and scenic valleys—making it a premier destination for paragliding enthusiasts and thrill-seekers alike.

It’s not just a sport—it’s a spiritual escape amidst nature and sky.

Paragliding Guidelines

  • Only trained and certified pilots are allowed for tandem flights.
  • Weather checks and wind conditions are mandatory before take-off.
  • Helmets, harnesses, and safety gear are compulsory.
  • Flights usually last 15–30 minutes depending on weather and package.
