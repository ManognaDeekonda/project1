About Us

"Sports in Bharath" is a project dedicated to promoting the rich and diverse sports culture of India. From ancient games like Kabaddi and Mallakhamb to modern champions excelling on global stages, Bharat's sporting spirit is unmatched. Our mission is to inspire youth, educate people about traditional and modern sports, and celebrate the achievements of Indian athletes through this interactive platform.
