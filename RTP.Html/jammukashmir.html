Ice Stock Sport

Frozen Precision from Jammu & Kashmir

About Ice Stock

Ice Stock, also known as Bavarian Curling, is a winter sport that is rapidly gaining popularity in Jammu & Kashmir. Played on ice with a heavy stock, players aim for accuracy and strategy—similar to curling.

Precision on Ice

J&K embraces Ice Stock as a sport of skill, balance, and freezing fun!

Jammu & Kashmir’s icy terrain makes it ideal for developing winter sports like Ice Stock, especially in Gulmarg and Pahalgam.

Why It’s Famous

Ice Stock combines winter environment, traditional sportsmanship, and growing international interest. With support from winter games federations, J&K is becoming India's Ice Stock hub.

Basic Rules

  • Each team has players who slide the stock toward a target (Daube).
  • The closest stock to the Daube scores points.
  • Strategy involves knocking opponent stocks away.
  • Played in rounds with alternate throws by teams.

Benefits of Playing

Winter Readiness

Builds balance, endurance, and cold-weather resilience.

Strategic Thinking

Like chess on ice—requires calculation and teamwork.

International Exposure

Ice Stock is played globally—opens opportunities for competition.
